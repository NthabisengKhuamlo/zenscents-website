About Me

Hi, I'm Nthabiseng, the proud founder of Zenscents. As a young mom and entrepreneur, I know what it's like to juggle everything—parenthood, business, and the dream of building something lasting. My passion for creating a business that reflects my values, love for my family, and the pursuit of excellence has always driven me.

At Zenscents, I wanted to bring something fresh, relatable, and authentic to the world of home fragrances. I’m inspired by the spirit of Gen Z—bold, vibrant, and always striving for more. I believe in offering high-quality products that are not only beautiful but also practical and accessible for everyone. My goal is to create a brand that resonates with people like me, those who understand the importance of both work and home life balance, and most importantly, those who believe in the power of scent to uplift any space.

Being a Black mom and entrepreneur comes with its own set of challenges, but it's also what motivates me every single day. Whether I'm spending time with my son, refining my business strategies, or collaborating with creatives, every moment counts. This journey is about growth, building memories, and sharing a bit of that energy with the world around me.

Thank you for supporting Zenscents—your journey to fresh, inspiring scents starts here!
